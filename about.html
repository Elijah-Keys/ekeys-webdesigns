About Us

A small studio built for small businesses

E Keys Creative Web Design started as a way to help promote creativity. We help give small business the tools they need to make a difference in a community and in the market. This is done through clean, simple, and effective web design that helps you connect with your customers.

I am Elijah, a student and web designer who cares about clear communication, simple user experience, and websites that actually help people find you. My focus is on small businesses in Sacramento, San Jose, and Chico that want professional websites that are customized to their preferences, at a price they can afford.

When we work together I listen first, then turn your story and goals into a site that feels like you. The result is a custom site that makes it easier for the right customers to trust and choose you.

Who I work best with

I work best with owners who care about their craft and the people they serve. You bring the product and story, I help bring the customers.

  • Small business owners who care about their community
  • People who value clear, fast communication
  • Teams who can share honest goals, timelines, and budget

If this sounds like you, I would love to hear about your business and help you plan a site that fits your goals and your budget.
